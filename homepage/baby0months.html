0-Month-Old Baby Development

Developmental Milestones

  • Focuses on faces, especially caregivers.
  • Startles to loud noises (Moro reflex).
  • Responds to sounds and begins tracking with eyes.
  • Displays reflexes like sucking and grasping.

Source: CDC - Developmental Milestones

Feeding & Sleep

  • Feeds every 2-3 hours; breastfeeding or formula feeding on demand.
  • Sleeps around 16-17 hours daily, often in short naps.

Play & Interaction

  • Skin-to-skin contact builds trust and emotional connection.
  • Responds to soft voices and eye contact.
  • Soothing sounds and gentle rocking help regulate emotions.

Source: UNICEF - Early Childhood Development
