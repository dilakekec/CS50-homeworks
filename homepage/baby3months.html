1-Month-Old Baby Development

Developmental Milestones

  • May briefly lift head while on tummy.
  • Begins to make cooing or vowel-like sounds.
  • Starts to visually track objects within close range.
  • Recognizes familiar voices and smells.

Source: CDC - Developmental Milestones (1 Month)

Feeding & Sleep

  • Feeds 8-12 times a day if breastfeed; formula-fed on babies may feed every 3-4 hours .
  • Begins to have longer sleep stretches at night (3-4 hours).

Source:

strong> AAP - HealthyChildren.org

Play & Interaction

  • Talks back with soft coos when spoken to.
  • Enjoys high-contrast toys and black and white patterns.
  • Continued skin-to-skin contact supports emotional development.

Source: UNICEF - Baby's First Month
