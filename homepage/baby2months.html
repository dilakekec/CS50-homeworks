2-Month-Old Baby Development

Developmental Milestones

  • Begins to smile at people(social smile).
  • Can briefly calm themselves (e.g., sucking on hand).
  • Makes cooing sounds and turns head toward sounds.
  • Holds head up and starts to push up when lying on tummy.

Source: CDC - Developmental Milestones (2 Month)

Feeding & Sleep

  • Feeds every 2-4 hours, often with increased demand during growth spurts .
  • Sleeps 14-17 hours in a 24-hour period, but may still frequently at night.

Source:

strong> AAP - HealthyChildren.org

Play & Interaction

  • Engage in face-to-face interaction and talk to your baby often.
  • Encourage tummy time daily to strengthen neck and shoulder muscles.
  • Use gentle music and toys with contrasting colors to stimulate senses.

Source: UNICEF - Baby's Second Month
